About the Game

Idle Forge is a mobile idle RPG built with Flutter. Equip your hero with powerful weapons and armor crafted in your very own forge, then watch them battle through increasingly dangerous stages — no micromanagement required.

Collect resources, unlock rare blueprints, and come back to a stronger hero every time you open the app. With offline rewards, your progress never stops.

Features

⚔️Idle combat with auto-attacks & skills
🔨Crafting system — forge weapons, armor & more
🛒In-game shop with daily offers & upgrades
🏆Achievements & quests
💾Offline rewards — progress while away
🌍German & English language support
